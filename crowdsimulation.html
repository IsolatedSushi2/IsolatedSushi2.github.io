Crowd Simulation

Description

This project focused on simulating crowd movement within real 3D urban environments by integrating multiple crowd simulation tools with public cadastral data and accurate road maps sourced from government websites. By querying the locations and forms of buildings and incorporating detailed infrastructure layouts, the simulation provided realistic visualisations of crowd dynamics in actual cityscapes.
